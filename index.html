Our Story

Born from a passion for clean eating

Bowlistry started in a small Thane kitchen in 2022 with one mission: prove that healthy food can be ridiculously delicious.

Our founder, Neha Kapoor, a certified nutritionist and fitness enthusiast, was tired of choosing between taste and nutrition. She assembled a team of chefs and dietitians, and together they created a menu where every calorie has a purpose — without sacrificing flavour.

From humble beginnings with 20 orders a day, Bowlistry now fuels over 1,500 meals daily across Thane and Mumbai. Every bowl is made fresh to order, every ingredient is sourced within 48 hours, and every recipe is nutritionist-approved.

Our Mission
To make eating clean as effortless and enjoyable as possible — one bowl at a time.
Our Vision
A healthier India, one household at a time. We aim to become the most-trusted health food brand in every metro city.
